What is ATT Enterprises?

ATT Enterprises is in essence the corporate entity of one individual's creative studio.
A specialist in full-stack design, photography and visual communications,
my philosophy is heavily focused on the study of human behavior
& the use of media to effectively create action, stimulate thought
or influence behaviour in an audience.
This philosophy however, is built on a stronger yet simpler foundation.
The desire to create work that tells a story.
